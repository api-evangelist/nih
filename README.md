# National Institutes of Health (NIH)

The US National Institutes of Health (NIH) provides a suite of free public REST APIs for biomedical research, clinical trial data, chemical compound databases, genomic datasets, and research funding information.

## APIs

- **NCBI E-utilities** - Programmatic access to all Entrez databases including PubMed, Gene, Nuccore, and Protein (https://eutils.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/entrez/eutils/)
- **NCBI Datasets v2** - Gene, genome, taxonomy, and virus datasets via OpenAPI 3.0 spec (https://api.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/datasets/v2/)
- **PubChem PUG REST** - Chemical compound data, structure search, and bioassay access (https://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/rest/pug/)
- **ClinicalTrials.gov REST API** - Clinical study data including eligibility, locations, and results (https://clinicaltrials.gov/api/v2/)
- **NIH RePORTER API** - NIH research funding, grants, publications, and patents (https://api.reporter.nih.gov/v2/)
- **NCBI BLAST URL API** - Sequence alignment job submission and result retrieval (https://blast.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/blast/Blast.cgi)
- **PubMed Central OA API** - Open Access content, ID conversion, and file services (https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/)
